Silent Hill 2 Review

Summary of the review

  • With Silent Hill 2, Konami has delivered a deep, long (10-15 hours) adventure that's scary in a disturbing, eerie fashion.
  • The game's mechanics show an attempt to improve the genre's general failings, and more importantly don't get in the way of the game or the story itself.
  • Konami's survival-horror game doesn't break the genre's mold so much as it modifies and eases up the rigid boundaries set early in the genre's early games.
