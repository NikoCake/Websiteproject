Resident Evil 4 Tips and Tricks

Tips and Tricks

  • In Resident Evil 4 Remake, there is a wide variety of weapons to use. These can be bought from the Merchant, found in certain locations, or unlocked by completing various puzzles. Better yet, some of them have excellent Exclusive Upgrades that can help them pack an extra punch when you use them next.
  • Speaking of weapons that pack a punch, Red9 is a fantastic addition to your inventory and can be found fairly early on in the game, too. After defeating Del Lago in Chapter 3, you can find this weapon in a treasure chest in the middle of the lake.
  • Alongside the Red9, the Broken Butterfly Magnum also has an incredible amount of Power. It actually has the highest Power in the game once its Exclusive Upgrade is applied (which increases it 1.5x).
