Larian Reveals How Many Copies of Baldur's Gate 3 Have Been Sold at The Vatican

Key Takeaways

  • Larian's Director of Publishing, Michael Douse, has confirmed that two copies of Baldur's Gate 3 have been sold in The Vatican, with another copy added to someone's wishlist.
  • Douse joked that the Pope may have wishlisted a copy for when he has a free minute.
  • The recently released PS5 Pro Enhanced version of Baldur's Gate 3 brings several graphical enhancements to the popular title.
