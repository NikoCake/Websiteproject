Stardew Valley Tips and Tricks

Tips and Tricks

  • Crops can only be grown in dirt patches, so you'll want to ensure you're picking a farm layout with plenty of yellow dirt across its map. Usable grass patches (not the flat green patches, but the fluffy stuff you'll walk through!), also only grow in dirt spaces, so if you want to raise a variety of animals, picking a map with lots of dirt helps ensure they have a steady food supply.
  • Almost every farm type has a particular specialization. For example, although you have the option to have monsters spawn on the farm in the new game menu, the Wilderness farm spawns monsters relative to your combat experience. All farm layouts still have space for growing some crops, but you don't have to make it your primary source of income.
  • When it's 1:30 AM and you've just arrived on the south side of your farm, you'll want as direct a path to your home and bed as possible! You'll pass out at 2 AM and owe a fee to whoever finds you.
