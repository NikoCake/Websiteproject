Call of Duty: Black Ops 6 Reveals Wild New Skins Coming to the Store

Key Takeaways

  • Premium skin bundles with special effects and weapon blueprints are coming in the first season of Black Ops 6.
  • A new Battle Pass in Black Ops 6 Season 1 will offer rewards, skins, and a new playable Operator, Goliath.
  • Alongside the Battle Pass, 9 premium Tracer Packs will be added to the in-game store, featuring Ultra Skins with unique themes.
