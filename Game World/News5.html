Horizon Zero Dawn Remastered Isn't Doing Very Well on PS5

Key Takeaways

  • Horizon Zero Dawn Remastered is among the least played PS5 console exclusives launched in 2024, as per a new report.
  • During its launch week, Horizon Zero Dawn Remastered saw nearly 92% fewer PS5 players than The Last of Us Part 2 Remastered.
  • Even on PC, Horizon Zero Dawn Remastered was outperformed by the original game, further indicating lackluster sales.
