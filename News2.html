Dead by Daylight Update Brings Back Popular Game Mode

Key Takeaways

  • Dead by Daylight reintroduces the beloved 2v8 mode, allowing players to enjoy new mechanics and activities.
  • The update includes new Killers to select, along with four distinct Killer classes and two additional realms.
  • Players can also expect exclusive cosmetic rewards and an Event Tome, but may encounter matchmaking issues due to high demand.
